Refund Policy

Refund &
Cancellation Policy

Our transparent refund policy ensures fair treatment for all customers. Please review our guidelines carefully before making a purchase.

1. Policy Overview

At EitronKYC, we strive to provide the best document preparation services. This Refund and Cancellation Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be issued and services may be cancelled.

Please note that once document processing has begun, service fees become non-refundable as we incur costs and dedicate resources to your case immediately.

2. Refund Eligibility

You may be eligible for a refund under the following circumstances:

  • Cancellation within 24 hours of purchase (before processing begins)
  • Service is unavailable for your specific case or location
  • Technical issues on our end prevent service delivery
  • Duplicate payment was made accidentally
  • Service was not delivered as described

Full Refund: Available if cancellation is made within 24 hours and no processing has begun.

3. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds are NOT available in the following situations:

  • After 24 hours from purchase or after processing has begun
  • Government rejection of your application (we don't control decisions)
  • Incomplete or incorrect information provided by you
  • Failure to submit required documents on time
  • Change of mind after service initiation
  • Government processing delays (these are beyond our control)

Important: Government fees are always non-refundable as they are paid directly to government authorities.

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team at support@eitronkyc.com
  2. Provide your order number and reason for refund
  3. Submit any required documentation
  4. Wait for our review (typically 3-5 business days)
  5. Receive refund decision via email

Approved refunds are processed within 5-10 business days and will be credited to your original payment method.

5. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be available in certain circumstances:

  • If only part of the service was delivered
  • If we agree to cancel after partial processing
  • If you upgrade to a different service
  • As a goodwill gesture for service issues

Partial refund amounts are determined on a case-by-case basis based on the work completed and resources utilized.

6. Service Cancellation

You can cancel your service under these conditions:

Within 24 Hours

Full refund available if no processing has begun

After 24 Hours

No refund if processing has started

To cancel, email us immediately with your order number. Cancellations are processed based on the timestamp of your request.

7. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with our refund decision:

  1. Respond to our decision email with detailed explanation
  2. Provide additional supporting documentation
  3. Request escalation to senior management
  4. Allow 5-7 business days for review

We aim to resolve all disputes fairly and promptly. Final decisions are made by our management team and are binding.

8. Need Help?

If you have questions about our refund policy or need to request a refund:

Email: support@eitronkyc.com

Phone: +1 (555) 123-4567

Hours: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST

Response Time: Within 24 business hours

Tip: Keep all correspondence and documentation related to your order for faster refund processing.
